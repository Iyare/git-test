The life of Dr Myles

Dr Miles was born on the 20th of April, 1954, to a poor family based in Nassua, Bahamas. He encountered Jesus in his teenage years and went on to serve the Lord in increasing capacities.

As a Bahamian evangelist and ordained minister, professor, author, speaker and leadership consultant. He founded and led the Bahamas Faith Ministries International (BFMI), and Myles Munroe International (MMI). He was also the chief executive officer and chairman of the board of the International Third World Leaders Association, and president of the International Leadership Training Institute. Dr Munroe was the author of numerous books which included;

  • Single, Married, Separated, and Life After Divorced
  • Understanding the Purpose and Power of Men
  • Understanding the Purpose and Power of a Woman
  • The Most Important Person on Earth
  • Purpose and Power of Love in Marriage
  • The Fatherhood Principle
  • Myles Munroe on Relationship
  • The Power of Freedom
  • Waiting and not Dating
